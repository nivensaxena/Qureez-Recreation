We’re a team that shares a core belief: the future belongs to those who can build it.That’s why we

Our approach

We don’t just invest in engineers, we’re creating a system that invests in them too. We work with engineers to start and build robust engineering companies that become training grounds for future engineer-founders.

All about people

We may operate in a highly technical field, but we’re driven by a simple truth: everything comes down to people.

Engineering at the core

Engineering is the foundation of the most meaningful companies and the key to solving the most interesting problems.

Driven by impact

Our idea of impact is helping others make theirs. We want to construct outcomes that couldn’t otherwise emerge.

Long-term thinking

We know nothing of consequence is built overnight. We value continuous optimization over quick returns and measure impact in lifetimes, not years or decades.
